Newsletter - January 2002

Inevitably, it is over these first few months that I have very little to write for the Newsletter, so contributions are even more welcome. Alan hasn’t received any information about upcoming events yet, although no doubt people will soon start distributing details. There are a few dates in the Jaguar Driver Calendar though, so keep your eye on that.

Our Area A.G.M. will be on 18th March at 8.00 p.m. Don’t forget to speak to Alan if there is any aspect of the club that you wish to be discussed. Also if there is anyone who wishes to take over the jobs done by Alan, Barbara, Roy and Kim and myself, let Alan know beforehand. In addition, bear in mind that it is at the A.G.M. that the John Dyall Trophy will be presented again to the person you think has contributed most to the Area during the last year. Voting slips for this will be issued with the February Newsletter.

The two Breakfast Runs to Rickling Green have been booked for 21st April and 6th October. Barbara has started collecting names for the first of these, so let her know if you want to attend.

Quite a few of us enjoyed a visit to the World War Two code breaking centre at Bletchley Park in 2000. Apparently the Whitewebbs Museum is organising a day out to the same place on Sunday 7th July. Several people in Area 57 are EDVVS members so more details will be known later.
