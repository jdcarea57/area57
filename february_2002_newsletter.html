Newsletter - February 2002

Please remember that the Area A.G.M. will be on 18th March at 8 p.m.   Due to other commitments Alan Collins has not been able to attend recent meetings so if there are any matters that you need to raise with him about the club, you will need to ring him.   Don’t forget to make use of your voting slips for this year’s John Dyall Trophy, which will be awarded at the A.G.M. for services to the Area during the past year.

We have been sent details of a Back To The Fifties Day at Waltham Cross on Saturday 8th June which is being organised by the Borough of Broxbourne. They do say that space is limited but I don’t know whether it is limited to 1950’s cars.

Other confirmed dates are the Enfield Pageant on 1st, 2nd and 3rd June, where we will put on our display on the Sunday & Monday, as usual, and also what seems to have become our first show of the season, namely the Herts Breast Unit Appeal at Stanborough Lakes on Mayday Bank Holiday, 6th May.

The enjoyable Steam and Country Show at Holly Hill Farm will once again be held during the August Bank Holiday, 25th and 26th.

PAINT MATCHING SERVICE

Report by John Wilkinson

A new computer based paint matching service is available at Car Spares, Delamare Road, Cheshunt, EN8 9AP. All you need is a sample panel from the car (minimum size about 5 inches square and more or less flat). The computer looks at a photo of the colour, mixes the correct sample which is sprayed on to a card, is allowed to dry and both the sample and the panel are compared to ensure a correct match. This is again done by the computer. The cost (at time of writing) is £25 plus paint. The contact is Bob on 01992-635585.
