Newsletter - November 2005
 
Just one last reminder for this year’s Christmas Dinner; it will be held at 7.30 p.m for 8.00 at Pearse House, Parsonage Lane, Bishops Storford CM23 5BQ.  Fancy dress is optional.
 
Well over thirty of us joined Tom Jones and his family and friends to celebrate his 80th birthday recently. It was nice to meet up with some of our less regular members on what was a very pleasant afternoon.

We are still talking about our twentieth celebrations next year. Another suggestion has been the Elizabethan banquet at Hatfield House. It doesn’t look as though a train trip is viable as they are all on week days.

The Jaguar Drivers’ Club is thirty years ahead of us and their 50th celebrations are centred at Heythrop Park near Banbury and Chipping Norton on 20th/21st May weekend. It has been several years since we have been to a big J.D.C. event as a club, so it might be worth attending on the Sunday to mark both anniversaries.

At the last meeting we discussed the possibility of having an occasional guest speaker at our meetings. While this is something to be welcomed in theory, there are a few problems. The first is our venue; while the main bar at the Legion is ideal for our usual meetings, the presence of other Legion members would make it impractical for a talk. The large room upstairs is usually available but in the winter is rather chilly and unwelcoming and, with the numbers we usually get, we tend to rattle around a bit up there. It’s also not feasible to relocate to the Whitewebbs Museum for any such occasion, as we are advertised as holding meetings at the Legion on the 1st and 3rd Mondays of the month. Richard knows someone who might be prepared to run a car film show on some occasion. It is a subject that we need to look into further but as always any suggestions to improve the club are always welcome.
